06 — Case Study
Automotive & Service

50% faster diagnostics. 41% fewer escalations. 11 weeks to production.

The Challenge

Technicians at 1,500+ dealers scattered repair procedures across PDFs, legacy portals, and wikis — driving inconsistent first-time fix rates and unnecessary warranty costs. Previous chatbots hallucinated specs and raised safety & liability concerns.

The Solution

Production RAG pipeline on AWS Bedrock with Anthropic Claude — backed by 60,000+ technical manuals in Amazon OpenSearch. Guardrails for grounding & safety. 250-question evaluation harness. Staged rollout: 40 technicians across 3 dealers.

07 — Case Study
Logistics & Marine

55% less analyst effort. Human decision authority. Zero black-box risk.

The Challenge

Analysts at this century-old logistics company assembled network data from TMS, WMS, rate engines, and spreadsheets manually — spending far more time compiling than optimizing. Leadership wanted efficiency gains without surrendering decision authority to opaque automation.

The Solution

Sidecar copilot architecture using OpenAI — read-only recommendations surfaced directly inside existing analyst dashboards. On-demand analysis and scheduled network monitoring. Humans retain full decision authority; the copilot surfaces insights and prioritizes, never commits.
